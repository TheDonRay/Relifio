## üéØ Why Relifio?

Mental health support shouldn't feel clinical or disconnected. Relifio was built with the belief that:
- **Your story matters** - Every conversation is a piece of your journey worth preserving
- **Growth is ongoing** - Emotional wellness isn't linear; it's a continuous process
- **Privacy is paramount** - Your thoughts and feelings deserve a safe, judgment-free space
- **AI can be empathetic** - Technology can provide meaningful support when designed with care
